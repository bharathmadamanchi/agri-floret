Scholarships

Follow your dreams with a little help from Floret

I spent the first few years learning how to grow flowers largely through trial and error. I failed way more than I succeeded and for the first few years, I really thought about quitting every single day. I spent so many hours walking circles in our tiny garage while making thousands of mixed bouquets, waiting for the day I could finally leave the farm and go learn from others. Every time a class or workshop was announced my heart would ache because I just couldn’t afford it.

My future changed the day I got an email from one of my floral heroes, offering me a slot at an upcoming workshop. Having someone who I admired so deeply believe in me helped me believe in myself. That act of generosity felt like getting permission to finally go after all of the dreams that I had put on hold for so long.

I have tried my best to infuse her generous legacy into everything we do and our scholarship program is my very favorite way of paying it forward. Hear the full story below.

Floret Scholarship Program

A needs-based scholarship for individuals & organizations

Since hosting our very first on-farm workshop in 2013, we’ve awarded hundreds of full and partial scholarships to individuals and organizations all around the world. Scholarship winners from diverse backgrounds and circumstances have taken what they’ve learned from the workshop to achieve their goals and give back to their communities in big ways and small.  

This year we planned on awarding 20 scholarships, but a donor just came forward and offered to sponsor another student, bringing the new total to 21! Each scholarship recipient will receive tuition-free enrollment in the course, which starts in January.

Applications will be accepted September 20–27, 2022. The deadline to apply is Tuesday, September 27 at 5 p.m. PDT. 

Winners will be notified by email and announced on the website on October 13.  

 

IMPORTANT DETAILS:

Scholarships are available to people of all races, ages, gender identities, and education levels. 

Applicants from anywhere in the world are welcome to apply.

Previous applicants are strongly encouraged to re-apply. 

Applications are welcome on behalf of another person.

 

HOW TO APPLY:

The application process is simple. In addition to providing some basic details about yourself or your organization, please be prepared to provide short answers (250 words or less) to the following:

Tell us about yourself, your flower business, organization, or cause. Please share any additional details that may be relevant to your application (i.e. community impact, financial need, local wages, currency conversion, etc.).

What interests you most about the Floret Online Workshop? How will the workshop help you accomplish your goals?

Scholarships will be awarded to applicants who best illustrate how the course will allow them to achieve their goals and clearly show how they will continue to pay it forward.

Danielle’s Story

At 8 months pregnant, Danielle planted her first cutting garden. Two weeks later she gave birth to her daughter Sammie and learned that she had Down syndrome.

“As a result of the lack of resources provided by the hospital, we started a nonprofit called Rising Kites, which distributes bags of encouragement and education to parents who have just learned that their child has Down syndrome.”

After taking the workshop, Danielle learned to efficiently make and sell bouquets to help fund the family’s nonprofit. As Rising Kites grows, it is Danielle’s hope that the organization will be able to provide jobs to individuals with disabilities who often have difficulty finding careers.

Danielle Grandholm | Rising Kites
Michigan, United States Floret Online Workshop Class of 2022

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • When is the scholarship application period?

    Applications will be accepted September 20–27, 2022. The deadline to apply is September 27 at 5 p.m. PDT. Because of the volume of applications we receive and our limited staff, we cannot accept late entries.

  • What is included with the scholarship?

    Each scholarship includes tuition-free enrollment in the Floret Online Workshop, our 6-week online course focused on growing flowers on a small scale that starts in January.

  • What is the cost of the Floret Online Workshop?

    Tuition for the Floret Online Workshop is $1,997 USD. We also offer a payment plan option of $379/month for 6 months.

  • I don't yet have a flower business. Can I still apply for a scholarship?

    While applications are welcome from all skill levels, scholarships will be awarded to those who best illustrate how the course will allow them to achieve their goals and clearly show how they will continue to pay it forward.

  • I don't live in the U.S. Am I eligible to apply for a scholarship?

    Applicants from anywhere in the world are encouraged to apply.

  • How many scholarships do you offer?

    Floret will award a total of 20 full scholarships to the 2023 Floret Online Workshop.

  • How can I donate a scholarship so another person can participate in the Floret Online Workshop?

    Thank you so much for your interest in donating a scholarship! For more information, please email [email protected].
